College of

Educational Vision

The College of Architecture and Arts is designed as a living laboratory for creativity. The campus architecture itself serves as a teaching tool, exposing structural elements and utilizing a diverse palette of materials to inspire students daily.

Central to the design is the "Creative Spine"—a multi-level soaring atrium that connects studios, workshops, and galleries. This space encourages accidental meetings and cross-pollination between different artistic disciplines, from industrial design to fine arts.
